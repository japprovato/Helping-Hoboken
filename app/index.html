About Helping Hoboken

When it is too difficult for people to find places to volunteer at, people usually don't bother giving back to their communities.

We recognize that sometimes it is just too hard to find volunteering opportunities in your community.

We've created this site to connect volunteers with those needing an extra set of hands. Volunteers and organizations needing volunteers can create accounts and gain access to our listings.

Additionally, we tabulate the total volunteer hours for our volunteers and will generate a report for he or she to submit to their organization's community service chair -- or to be impressed with their hard work!
